Mariachi Herencia

We are Mariachi Herencia de Cuco Del Cid. Under the direction of maestro Refugio "Cuco" Del Cid, Mariachi Herencia is one of the premier mariachi groups in Tucson, AZ. Mariachi Herencia performs at all types of events both in Tucson, AZ as well as all of southern Arizona and we would like to officially make our services available for you. No matter the occasion, Mariachi Herencia can assure you that our music will add the perfect touch for your event. Contact Mariachi Herencia in Tucson, AZ today and ask us about our services, prices, and availability.

Hear us live

If you would like to see us perform live in Tucson, AZ, Mariachi Herencia donates their time and talent every Sunday (excluding the 5th Sunday of the month) at Most Holy Trinity Parish for the 11am mass. We also play twice for the parish's Easter and Christmas masses. You can find Most Holy Trinity at the corner of Speedway and Greasewood.
